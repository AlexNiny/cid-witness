# Product Context

## Product

CID Witness is a small Filecoin-powered evidence composer for the FilecoinTLDR Builder Challenge. It helps a builder, archivist, or community reviewer turn a Filecoin piece CID, deal id, provider id, client id, or IPFS CID into a concise verification packet.

## Users

- First-time Filecoin builders who need a working demo with real Filecoin interaction.
- Judges reviewing whether a project uses Filecoin meaningfully.
- People sharing storage proofs publicly and needing a readable summary.

## Product Purpose

Make Filecoin storage market data understandable at the moment of use. The main mechanic is: enter a CID or deal identifier, fetch live Filecoin market data, calculate status and timing, then generate a clear shareable evidence note.

## Tone

Precise, calm, proof-oriented. Avoid hype and avoid investment language.

## Anti-References

- No crypto trading dashboards.
- No decorative neon, glassmorphism, or generic web3 landing page tropes.
- No fake upload flow that pretends to store data without a real backend.
